PAKISTAN UNITED WORKERS FEDERATION (PUWF)


INSTRUMENT
Between


1) Pakistan United Workers Federation (PUWF) here in referred as first party.


2)
Second Party (Name of The Union)


This Instrument of Federation is executed and signed between the above two parties.
WHEREAS both the parties have a common cause of strengthening unity amongst the
working class particularly to safeguard, promote and strengthen their relation and
affiliation with PUWF. So the parties after careful consideration have agreed to join
hands subject to the following terms and conditions: -


Terms and Conditions


1. The proposal of affiliation of the aforesaid Union with the aforesaid Federation
was presented in the meeting of the union details as stipulated in its attached
Resolution. The Constitution of the Federation was also presented in the
meeting. The constitutional meeting of the union consented with the aims and
objects of the Federation and resolved that the Union be affiliated with the
aforesaid Federation (first party).


2. The Union (second party) undertake to fulfill the following obligations arising
from their affiliation to the Federation as provided under Article 25 and it
Shall :-
(a) endeavor to further the aims and objects of the
Federation;
(b) pay the subscription and/or special dues;
(c) extend all possible help to other affiliated units/


constituents;


(d) supply any information asked for by the Secretariat of the
Federation and also furnish copy of the Annual Returns while
submitting the same to the Registrar of Trade Unions.
admit to their Executive/Managing/General Council meeting


(e) any person deputed by the Federation;


## Page 3

(f) give due consideration to the recommendations made by the
Federation; and


(g) carry out instructions and/or directions issued by the
Federation and implement its resolutions and /or decisions.


3. The Union agrees that the Federation has full right to represent the union
and its members.


4. The Federation affirms that it will carry out its affairs according to its
constitution and purely on democratic lines.


5. The Federation undertakes that before taking any decision in regard to the


Union, it will fully consider the view point of the Union.


The above Instrument has been read over microscopically and decided to join their
hands for the instrument.
