# Union Admin Portal Plan

## Screens

- Dashboard: member count, dues health, compliance deadlines, active grievances, CBA/election status.
- Members: searchable dense registry, member detail, Form C-aligned edit flow.
- Office Bearers: designation list, term status, outsider warning, history.
- Finance: dues ledger, employer remittance, receipts, welfare claim entry.
- Annual Return: Form L/J-style wizard and GS/FS approval states.
- Elections: schedule, nominations, candidate list, polling state, results.
- CBA/CoD: certificate status, expiry countdown, demand stages, negotiations.
- Grievances/Legal: queue, assignment, status update, escalation, hearings.
- Documents/Compliance: DMS, legal packets, document expiry, obligations, evidence links.

## UX Rules

- Union admin screens are dense operational tools, not promotional pages.
- Tables become mobile lists with filters, quick actions, and detail sheets.
- Compliance risk and legal deadlines must be visually prominent.
- Every form must cite the mapped source in comments or field metadata.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Union admin can inspect all core operational modules from dashboard quick actions.
- Member list, office bearer list, grievance queue, and annual return states render from mock data.
- Annual return, office bearer, and member modules follow legal field mapping.
