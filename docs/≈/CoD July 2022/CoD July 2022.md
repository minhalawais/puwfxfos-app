THIS MEMORANDUM OF SETTLEMENT is made at Kot Addu on August 22, 2022 between
Management of Kot Addu Power Company Limited, a company incorporated under the laws of
Pakistan (hereinafter referred to as the “Management”, which expression shall mean its successors
and assigns) of the one part; AND Al-Takbeer KAPCO Employees Union (registered) Collective
Bargaining Agent (hereinafter referred as the “CBA” or as “Al-Takbeer”, which expression shall
include its successors and assigns) of the other part and all the existing and future regular workers
(Staff) employed by the Management in Kot Addu Power Company Limited (hereinafter referred to as
the “Establishment” or the “Company”).


WHEREAS
1. Al-Takbeer was elected as the Collective Bargaining Agent of the Establishment at the
Referendum held on July 22, 2022.
6 2. Al-Takbeer submitted a Charter of Demands to the Management vide letter bearing No.


ALT/KAPCO/CBA/002 dated July 25, 2022 (hereafter referred to as “Charter of Demands” or
“COD”), which is in accordance with Section 34 of Punjab Industrial Relations Act, 2010 and
Rules made thereunder (Copy of the Charter of Demands is attached as Annexure-A).


3. The Management and CBA held a number of meetings to discuss and bargain the Charter of
Demands. After a series of bilateral negotiations, a final meeting was held on August 22, 2022,
to discuss and bargain on the Charter of Demands to arrive at an amicable settlement.


4. The Management (for and on behalf of the Company) and the CBA have agreed to enter into
this Memorandum of Settlement based on terms and conditions agreed upon, which shall be
considered as full and final settlement of all demands made or specified directly or indirectly(\
or raised in the Charter of Demands. /

TERMS OF THE SETTLEMENT/AGREEMENT


It has been agreed as follows:


1. A total 11% increase in basic salary, without compounding (6% increase as Charter of
Demands and 5% annual increment) will be given to Staff (KSG-8 to KSG-1) w.e.f. July 1, 2022.


Appropriate number of graduate children of existing Staff and as per agreed criteria will be


given training in year 2022-23 at a suitable time considering the availability of training
facilities in the Company.


3. Itis agreed that appropriate numbers of Staff shall be promoted w.e.f July 1, 2022.


4. TA/DA rates would be revised.


5. Management will give an option to each eligible staff a lumpsum amount in lieu of post-
retirement medical and electricity facilities, based on actuarial valuation as on 30" June of
preceding year of retirement for said employee. It will be full and final settlement and no
further claim would be entertained in this regard.


Al-Takbeer agrees on behalf of all workers (Staff) to help and to extend full co-operation in the
maintenance of good conduct and discipline in the Establishment. The CBA recognizes that the
Management has the right and sole responsibility to operate the Company and conduct its business
and initiate direction in accordance with Company Rules and Regulations (as amended from time to
time) and applicable laws.


Management and Al-Takbeer have signed this Memorandum of Settlement and have agreed and
accepted that this Memorandum records the full and final settlement of all and any other demands
raised by the CBA in the Charter of Demands ; and any other industrial disputes whatsoever in
existence up to the date of signing of this Memorandum of Settlement and all other demands
whether included or not in the Charter of Demands , which shall be deemed to have been settled,
